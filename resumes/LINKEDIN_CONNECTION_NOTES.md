## Tips for LinkedIn Connection Notes

1. **Keep it under 300 characters** - LinkedIn's limit
2. **Be specific** - Mention relevant experience
3. **Show value** - What you bring to the connection
4. **Include location** - Shows you're serious about relocating
5. **Add website link** - Makes it easy for them to learn more
6. **Be genuine** - Authentic connections work best
7. **Use their name** - Personalization matters
