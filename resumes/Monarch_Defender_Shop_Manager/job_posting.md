# Shop Manager – Luxury Classic Car Restoration
## Monarch Defender LLC

**Location:** Grayslake, IL (On-site)  
**Job Type:** Full-time  
**Pay:** $80,000 - $105,000 per year

---

## Company Overview

Monarch Defender is a world-class automotive atelier specializing in the complete restoration and modernization of classic Land Rover Defenders and other heritage vehicles. Each project is hand-built from the ground up—blending timeless design, modern engineering, and meticulous craftsmanship.

The Grayslake, IL facility serves as the flagship workshop, where bespoke vehicles are created for discerning clients around the world.

---

## Position Overview

Experienced and highly organized Shop Manager to oversee operations at the restoration and manufacturing facility. This role is ideal for a professional who thrives in a high-end, detail-driven environment and understands the standards of excellence expected in luxury automotive craftsmanship.

The Shop Manager will coordinate the daily workflow, manage a team of skilled technicians, and ensure each project meets Monarch's uncompromising quality benchmarks.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Supervise and coordinate all workshop operations, from mechanical restoration to final assembly
- Manage project timelines, team schedules, and production priorities
- Maintain strict quality control standards and ensure all vehicles meet Monarch's brand expectations
- Oversee parts procurement, vendor communication, and inventory management
- Collaborate closely with the design, engineering, and leadership teams to meet project milestones
- Lead and motivate a team of skilled craftsmen and technicians
- Maintain shop organization, safety compliance, and a professional workspace that reflects the brand's values
