## Tips for Using These Messages

1. **Personalize**: Always customize with the recipient's name and any specific details about them or your shared connection
2. **Be Concise**: Keep messages brief - people are busy
3. **Clear Call to Action**: Make it clear what you're asking for (opportunities, connections, advice)
4. **Timing**: Send messages during business hours for better response rates
5. **Follow Up**: If someone views your profile but doesn't respond, wait a week and send a brief follow-up
6. **Value First**: Consider offering value or insights before asking for something
7. **Be Genuine**: Use your authentic voice - these are templates, but make them yours
