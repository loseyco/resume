## Tips for Posting

### Best Times to Post:
- **LinkedIn:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-9am or 12-1pm (your timezone)
- **Facebook:** Tuesday-Thursday, 1-3pm
- **Twitter:** Monday-Friday, 8-9am or 5-6pm

### Engagement Tips:
1. **Respond quickly** to comments and messages
2. **Share updates** - "Thanks for 500 views on my resume!"
3. **Engage with others** - comment on posts in your industry
4. **Post regularly** - 2-3 times per week, vary the content
5. **Use relevant hashtags** - mix broad and specific
6. **Tag appropriately** - tag companies/organizations you mention (if relevant)

### Hashtag Suggestions:
- `#RemoteWork`
- `#OpenToWork`
- `#SoftwareEngineer`
- `#WebDeveloper`
- `#ElectronicsEngineer`
- `#React`
- `#Supabase`
- `#Vercel`
- `#RacingEngineer`
- `#Hiring`
- `#TechJobs`
- `#SoftwareDevelopment`
- `#CareerChange`
- `#IndyCar`
- `#Racing`

### Follow-Up Strategy:
- Week 1: Post 1 (Direct & Professional)
- Week 2: Post 3 (Story-Driven)
- Week 3: Post 5 (Tech-Stack Focused)
- Week 4: Post 2 (Achievement-Focused)
- Then rotate and vary
