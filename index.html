About Me

Skilled and experienced electronics engineer and manager with a proven track record across multiple industries. Extensive background in medical equipment engineering, professional racing, and IT systems coordination.

My experience spans from medical equipment engineering at Siemens Healthineers, where I led teams and operated advanced Proton Therapy systems, to professional racing where I've served as trackside engineer, managed teams, handled data strategy, and coordinated IT systems for world-class racing teams including Honda Racing Corporation.
